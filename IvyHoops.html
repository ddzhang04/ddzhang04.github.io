Ivy Hoop Statistics

Ivy Hoop Statistics is a website that shows statistics and data about the Ivy Basketball League. Users are able to view the latest news, schedules, scores, standings, and rosters of the various Ivy League Basketball teams. Users are also able to follow their favorite teams and see the schedule for the teams that they follow. The news and data about teams was gathered through web scraping and overall this project was implemented using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, SQL, and Flask.
